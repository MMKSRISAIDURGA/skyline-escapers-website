Famous Places in Bali

Tanah Lot

A stunning sea temple perched on a rock formation, famous for its sunset views.

Tegallalang Rice Terraces

Scenic rice paddies offering breathtaking landscapes and a glimpse into traditional farming.

Uluwatu Temple

A clifftop temple with stunning ocean views, famous for its Kecak fire dance performances.

Ubud Monkey Forest

A lush sanctuary home to hundreds of monkeys and ancient temple ruins.
